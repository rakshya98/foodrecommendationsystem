FOODLAND :A FOOD INFORMATION AND RECOMMENDATION SYSTEM

Scope of The System

The food information and recommendation system is the solution designed to provide comprehensive information about popular South Asian food . It also aims to offer recommendations of food to users based on ratings . The system basically focuses to discover diverse culinary tradition and promote healthy,tasty,convenient food along with the suggestions of highly rated food based on user preferences.

  • FoodLand will be covering popular dishes from few South Asian nations.
  • FoodLand will be suggesting the food items based on popularity and collaborative filtering.
  • The food information will contain the nutrition level ( fat ,carbohydrate , protein and calories) of the dishes , ingredients and recipe .
